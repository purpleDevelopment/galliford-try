Back to basics

Back to Basics is a refocus. It is about concentrating on what is important to enable YOU to carry out YOUR tasks safely.

A significant proportion of incidents on Galliford Try sites are caused by either the wrong person, the wrong equipment, a lack of planning or site conditions which are not conducive to safe working. Simply put, Back to Basics will achieve a greater level of Health and Safety performance. Back to Basics has four key elements for you to ask yourselves:

  • The right person
  • The right equipment
  • The right planning
  • The right workplace

To work safely on a Galliford Try Group site, YOU must answer yes to all the above points. If the answer to one or more of the above is no, you have the authority of the Galliford Try Executive Chairman to stop work and speak to your Supervisor. YOU must not recommence until YOU can answer yes to all of the above points.
