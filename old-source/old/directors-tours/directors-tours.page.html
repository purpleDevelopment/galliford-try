Directors Tours

Each business unit will nominate their senior staff anually who will undertake Directors Tours. These tours provide senior members of staff the opportunity to demonstrate visible safety leadership.The Directors Tour booklet (LINK) provides guidance, hints and tips on how to undertake an effective visit. The booklet provides an optional table that can be completed to record the visit which concentrates on the key aspects of Back to Basics and Golden Rules.
