Embrace the challenge

Our Executive and Divisional Boards have fully endorsed the implementation of Challenging Beliefs, Affecting Behaviour across all operating companies within the Galliford Try Group to promote a culture that complements our robust HS&E Standards. Since 2012 the businesses have taken up that challenge and have further developed and implemented their own safety initiatives under the banner of Challenging Beliefs, Affecting Behaviour. This has achieved great historical success and has embedded our safety leadership programme into the culture of the business.

We now invite you to join us in playing your part and driving our health and safety culture of the business forward.

All Business Units will still be encouraged to develop and implement their own safety initiatives to complement this programme. However, this Toolkit aims to provide the necessary tools to ensure that this programme can be effectively facilitated.
