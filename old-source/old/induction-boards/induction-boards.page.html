Induction boards

Induction boards provide an innovative and interesting means of communicating health and safety information to new personnel to workplaces. The board is intended to reduce the amount of time inductions take and should be utilised in conjunction with other “informative” tools such as First Ten Minutes, SBD‘s, etc. Boards should be “personalised” to the respective site / workplace by using photographs / organograms / layout drawings, etc.
