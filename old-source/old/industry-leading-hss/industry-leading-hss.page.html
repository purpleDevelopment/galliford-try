An industry-leading health and safety strategy

Challenging Beliefs, Affecting Behaviour has been designed and maintained in conjunction with our operational teams to set the vision and values of the business, raise awareness of behavioural safety, challenge their beliefs on what can really be achieved within our organisation and thereafter affect the behaviours of our people to reduce the amount of harm and suffering experienced by them in the course of running a successful business.

The supporting Toolkit houses all the components of the ‘Challenging Beliefs, Affecting Behaviour’ Safety Leadership Programme. As with all toolkits the contents are to be used and adapted according to the environment in which they are to be used. The power of discretion as to what to use and when is a unique function of our programme which empowers site management to use only the tools they believe will maximise health and safety performance.
