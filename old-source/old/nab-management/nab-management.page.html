NAB management and delivery

Anyone who works on site for a minimum of two weeks can join a NAB Team. It is recommended that representatives from key subcontractors and the local Health and Safety Advisor should also be involved. The NAB Team will determine the positive behaviours expected and encourage ownership of safety by all persons on site. Agreement will be sought to ensure team members engage with all operatives to encourage safe behaviour, regardless of the direct management chain. Culture Change Workshops can be used in the delivery of NAB, both to explain key actions required and to train ‘change leaders’ on how to facilitate a safety excellence culture and commit to a personal safety action plan.
