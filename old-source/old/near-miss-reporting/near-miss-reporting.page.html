Near Miss Reporting

Near Miss Reporting and hazard recognition is an important tool in identifying failings before harm occurs. It is one of the main lines of defence against specific sources of risk. The Near Miss Observation (NeMO) Card is an effective behavioural tool which can stop complacency on site before accidents occur.

Near Miss Reporting is also invaluable in obtaining feedback as to the effectiveness of our safety culture and management system. Near misses and the early identification of hazards provide us with our ‘free warnings’ to take proactive actions to improve our working environment, by highlighting potential future accidents, incidents and management failures.
