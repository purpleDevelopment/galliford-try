Personal Commitment Statement

The Personal Commitment Statement is signed by individuals to confirm that they will challenge themselves and those who work for us to ensure that the highest possible standards of safety are achieved in everything that is done. They are also confirming that they will embrace the Galliford Try safety vision and concept of care to drive the Challenging Beliefs, Affecting Behaviour initiative.
