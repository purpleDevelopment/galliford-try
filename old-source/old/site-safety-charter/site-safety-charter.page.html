Site Safety Charter

The Site Safety Charter allows the site management team to develop site-specific goals and visually demonstrate senior management commitment to the Challenging Beliefs, Affecting Behaviour initiative. Sites are encouraged to develop their own site-specific safety commitments in addition to those shown below.

The Safety Charter Commitments

  1. Nothing we do is so important that we cannot take the time to do it safely.
  2. The safety of our employees, colleagues and the public is paramount.
  3. I will endeavour to help everyone I work with to work safely and prevent unsafe working practices.
  4. I will never knowingly walk past an unsafe act or condition.
  5. I am committed to the principle that all accidents are preventable.
  6. I will lead by example.
