Toolbox Talks

The Toolbox Talks play a major part in communicating our Challenging Beliefs, Affecting Behaviour initiative to operatives. A suite of Toolbox Talks has been developed to assist site management, coaches and contractors to reinforce the behavioural safety message to the workforce in bite-sized chunks, concentrating on key areas such as intervention and risk.The Toolbox Talk library will expand as the programme grows and as a result of future developments in behavioural safety, and feedback from NAB forums, site operatives and coaches.

Appendix 13 includes Toolbox Talks for Behavioural Safety, Safe Behaviour Discussions (SBDs) and Near Miss Reporting.
