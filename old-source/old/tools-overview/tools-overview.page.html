Tools and Resources - Overview

A suite of communication and implementation tools has been developed to assist senior management, operational teams, coaches and operatives to implement our ‘Challenging Beliefs, Affecting Behaviour’ Safety Leadership Programme throughout the Group. This suite of tools will develop as our behavioural culture evolves.

  • No Accident Behaviour (NAB) pack
  • Safe Behaviour Discussions (SBDs)
  • Near Miss Reporting
  • First Ten Minutes Briefing
  • Toolbox Talks
  • Posters
  • Site Safety Charter
  • Safety Shutdown Initiative
  • Safety Reward Schemes
  • Director Tours
  • GT:GO
  • Induction boards
  • Communication boards
