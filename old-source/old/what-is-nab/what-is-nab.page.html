What is NAB (No Accident Behaviour)?

No Accident Behaviour is a safety initiative project teams can use to declare and commit to their chosen safe behaviours, and agree to intervene when others do not act safely.

NAB has two main objectives:

  1. To further improve attitudes towards safety, bringing our culture to an industry-leading level of safety excellence.
  2. To empower subcontractors and the workforce to deliver a high level of personal safety performance.

NAB is designed to enable subcontractors, operatives and management to openly engage, take part in safety forums and have a direct and positive impact on the safety of all of those on site. Subcontractors and operatives are encouraged to participate in behavioural committees, feedback to site management and actively intervene on unsafe acts or conditions.
